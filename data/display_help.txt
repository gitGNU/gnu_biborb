<p>
This menu give access to an HTML representation of the bibliography.
</p>

<ul>
    <li><strong>All</strong>:  all entries are displayed by increasing id,</li>
    <li><strong>Groups</strong>: display entries of a given group,</li>
    <li><strong>Search</strong>: search the database for an entry (title, keywords, authors).</li>
</ul>

Several actions may be available for each displayed entry:

<ul>
    <li>Download a ps/ps.gz/pdf</li>
    <li>Access the abstract</li>
    <li>Get the BibTeX source code</li>
    <li>Go to a related webpage (Home pages)</li>
    <li>Go to a local page on the web server</li>
    <li>Edit the entry to update information (need authentication mode disabled or authenticated user)</li>
    <li>Remove the entry (need authentication mode disabled or authenticated user) <span style="color:red">No Warning!!</span></li>
    <li>Add the entry to the basket</li>
</ul>
