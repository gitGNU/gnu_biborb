This menu give access to the basket. The basket records entries you have selected in the bibliograpy.
<br/>
You can:
<ul>
    <li>display all entries in the basket,</li>
    <li>change the group of all entries in basket,</li>
    <li>reset groups of all entries in the basket,</li>
    <li>export the selection to BibTeX,</li>
    <li>export the selection to HTML.</li>
</ul>
