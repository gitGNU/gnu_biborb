<p>
This menu give access to the basket. The basket records entries you have selected in the bibliograpy to perform some actions on.
</p>


Actions that can be done on the basket are:
<ul>
    <li>Display all entries in the basket.</li>
    <li><img src="data/images/cvs-remove-16.png" border="0" alt="remove" align="center"/> Remove entries from the basket.</li>
    <li>Change the group of all entries in basket (classification).</li>
    <li>Reset groups of all entries in the basket.</li>
    <li>Export the selection to BibTeX.</li>
    <li>Export the selection to HTML.</li>
</ul>
